Hey, u up (to vote)?

Hall of fame responses to political text communications

WRITTEN BY JOSEPH RAMOS

No, these aren’t booty calls from Joe Biden (unless…?). Ahead of November’s election, students took to their keyboards and sent out hundreds of political messages. From Ted Cruz stans in Texas to civically-minded Florida Men to whoever it is that lives in Ohio, potential swing state voters received a slew of texts asking if they were registered to vote or willing to support certain candidates. While the vast majority of voters left students on read, a small percentage fired back with responses ranging from confrontational to downright confusing. Click through to see their replies and witness what technically counts as democracy at work.

Names of respondents have been removed to preserve anonymity
